Our Story

Built for women who wander

She Goes Outside was born from a simple belief: that fresh air, good company, and a gentle trail can change everything.

🌿
Meet the Founder

JoAnne Yorkston

Founder & Chief Adventure Planner

JoAnne has always believed that the outdoors should feel welcoming — not intimidating. After years of wishing there was a group for women who loved nature and exploring but didn't consider themselves athletes, she decided to create one herself.

She Goes Outside grew out of JoAnne's personal conviction that mid-life and beyond is the perfect time to discover (or rediscover) the joy of being outside. Whether it's a slow bike ride past a lavender farm or sipping tea while watching herons on a quiet lake, she plans every event with intention, warmth, and a little bit of wonder.

Based in the Pacific Northwest, JoAnne knows every scenic byway, hidden trailhead, and exceptional local bakery in the region — and she cannot wait to share them with you.

Our values

Inclusivity

Every woman is welcome. Every pace is valid. We go together.
